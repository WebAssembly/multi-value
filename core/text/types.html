Abbreviations¶

Multiple anonymous parameters or results may be combined into a single declaration:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef3159#1{{}}\mathdef3159{parameter} & \def\mathdef3231#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3231{(}~~\def\mathdef3232#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3232{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3233#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3233{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3234#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3234{(}~~\def\mathdef3235#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3235{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3236#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3236{)})^\ast \\ \def\mathdef3159#1{{}}\mathdef3159{result} & \def\mathdef3237#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3237{(}~~\def\mathdef3238#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3238{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3239#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3239{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3240#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3240{(}~~\def\mathdef3241#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3241{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3242#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3242{)})^\ast \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
